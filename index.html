Architecture Summary

What this product is designed to do.

Persistence Layer: Firestore stores resume profile, keywords, applications, and sync metadata per anonymous user.

Intelligence Layer: Gemini parses resume text, matches live postings, and detects lifecycle transitions from recruiter emails.

Presentation Layer: A minimalist Kanban-based UI reduces cognitive load while keeping the job search stateful and transparent.
